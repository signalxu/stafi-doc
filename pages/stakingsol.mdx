## How to Stake SOL with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**Phantom**:

- Download Phantom from the [Phantom Website](https://phantom.app/download).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create a new wallet or import your existing wallet.

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- This wallet is available as an extension on both Chrome and Firefox browsers.
- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for **[Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd)** or **[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create a new wallet or import your existing wallet.


<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [SOL staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/SOL?tokenStandard=Native) and click on the "Stake" button to stake your SOL.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolhowtostake_1.png)

### Input staking amount

To stake SOL, you will need to provide the desired amount of SOL to be staked and then click on the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolhowtostake_2.png)

### Confirm transaction

Sign the transaction using the Phantom wallet.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolhowtostake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

Once staking is completed, the operation will be processed successfully.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rbnbunstake_4.png)

</Steps>

## Unstake

<Callout type="info">
rSOL mint and burn in StaFi Chain, if you want to unstake
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [SOL unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/SOL?tokenStandard=Native) and click on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

To unstake SOL, you will need to provide the desired amount of BNB to be unstaked and then click on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolunstake_2.png)

### Confirm transaction

Provide your password and sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolunstake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

Once unstaking is completed, the operation will be processed successfully.

![StaFi Staked SOL](/image/rsolunstake_4.png)

</Steps>
