## How to Stake DOT with StaFi

### Required Wallets

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for **[Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd)** or **[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [DOT staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/DOT?tokenStandard=Native) and click on "Connect Wallet".

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_1.png)

### Connect wallets

Connect StaFi Chain and Polkdot wallet and confirm.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_2.png)

### Click stake and input stake amount

When wallets are connectd, click the "Stake" button and input the staking amount.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_3.png)

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_4.png)

### Confirm the transaction

Enter password to sign the transaction using your Polkadotjs wallet.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_5.png)

### Waiting for completed

Congratulations! Your DOT staking process has been successfully completed. You will now earn rewards for staking your DOT.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdothowtostake_6.png)


</Steps>

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [DOT unstaking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/DOT?tokenStandard=Native) and click on "Connect Wallet".

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdotunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

To unstake DOT, you will need to provide the desired amount of DOT to be unstaked and then click on the "Unstake" button.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdotunstake_2.png)

### Confirm the transaction

Enter password to sign the transaction using your Polkadotjs wallet.

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdotunstake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

The unstaking operation will be successfully completed. Your tokens will be sent to your wallet after the unbonding period has ended (~28days).

![StaFi Staked DOT](/image/rdotunstake_4.png)

</Steps>
