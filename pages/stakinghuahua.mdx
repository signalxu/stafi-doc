## How to Stake HUAHUA with StaFi

### Required Wallet

**Keplr**

- Download Keplr from the [Keplr Website](https://www.keplr.app/).
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

<Steps>

### Go to staking page and connect wallet

Enter the StaFiHub App, and connect your Keplr wallet to StaFiHub and CosmosHub.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahowtostake_1.png)

### Confirm connected wallets

Once connected, click on “Confirm”

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahowtostake_2.png)

### Select staking token

Click on “Stake HUAHUA on the rToken Dashboard.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahhowtostake_3.png)

### Input staking amount

Enter the desired amount of HUAHUA to be staked. Then, provide your StaFi Hub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of rHUAHUA to be received by clicking on the button.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahhowtostake_4.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show the amount of SWTH you are about to stake and its associated gas fees. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of HUAHUA. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

<Callout type="info">
Do not edit the memo shown on the Keplr transaction. Editing the memo will cause the transaction to fail.
</Callout>

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahowtostake_5.png)

### Wait for confirmations

Your transaction can be tracked through the notification bar. Upon success, your rHUAHUA will appear on the [rSWTH Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rHUAHUA/dashboard).

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahowtostake_6.png)

</Steps>

## Unstake

<Steps>

### Go to unstaking page

Go to the [rHUAHUA Dashboard](https://app.stafihub.io/rToken/rHUAHUA/stake), and click on “Redeem rHUAHUA” to start unstaking

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuahunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

Enter the desired amount of SWTH to be unstaked. Then, provide your StaFiHub address as the receiving address. Finally, confirm the amount of HUAHUA to be received by clicking on the “Unbond” button.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuaunstake_2.png)

### Confirm details

Confirm the following information on the pop-up page: the amount of unbonded rSWTH, Commission Fees (initially set at 0.2%), Relay Fee, Unbonding Period, and the number of HUAHUA received. Once confirmed, click on "unbond".

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuaunstake_3.png)

### Approve transaction

After clicking the button, you will be redirected to your Keplr wallet. The confirmation screen will show associated gas fees for the transaction. Please review the information carefully. To proceed, click on the "Approve" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuaunstake_4.png)

### Wait for confirmation

Upon successful unstaking, the amount of unbonding HUAHUA will be displayed on the rHUAHUA Dashboard under the “unbonded records” section. After the unbonding period ends, tokens will automatically be sent to the receiving address.

![StaFi Staked HUAHUA](/image/rhuahuaunstake_5.png)

</Steps>
