# How to Stake ETH with StaFi

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'

<Callout type="warning">
Please be aware that the rToken app does not support Ledger devices. Therefore, it is crucial that you do not connect the app using a Ledger device. Connecting the app with Ledger may result in the loss of your assets.
</Callout>

## Dependency

**Metamask**:

- Download Metamask from the **[Metamask Website](https://metamask.io/download.htm)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to import or create a new wallet address

## Usage

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [ETH staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/ETH?tokenStandard=ERC20) and click on the "Stake" button to stake your ETH.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/rETHhowtostake-1.png)

### Input staking amount

To stake ETH, you will need to provide the desired amount of ETH to be staked and then click on the "Stake" button. The minimum staking amount required is 0.01 ETH.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/rETHhowtostake-2.png)

### Confirm transaction

After clicking the "Stake" button, you will be redirected to your MetaMask wallet. In your MetaMask wallet, you will see a confirmation screen that shows the amount of ETH you are about to stake and the associated gas fee. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of ETH. If you are satisfied with the information, click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/rETHhowtostake-3.png)

### Waiting for completed

Congratulations! Your ETH staking process has been successfully completed. You will now earn rewards for staking your ETH.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/rETHhowtostake-4.png)

### Add rETH to your wallet

Navigate to the rETH information page and click on the "+" symbol. In the Metamask interaction window, select "Add token" to successfully include rETH in your wallet.

![StaFi Staked ETH](/image/rETHhowtostake-5.png)

</Steps>
