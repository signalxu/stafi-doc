# Staking BNB with StaFi

This document is intended for users and developers who want to learn more about StaFi Staked BNB and how to stake BNB using StaFi.

import { Callout } from 'nextra-theme-docs'
import { Steps } from 'nextra-theme-docs'


## How to Stake BNB with StaFi

<Callout type="info">
Please be aware that the rToken app does not support Ledger devices. Therefore, it is crucial that you do not connect the app using a Ledger device. Connecting the app with Ledger may result in the loss of your assets.
</Callout>

### Required Wallets

**Metamask**:

- Download Metamask from the **[Metamask Website](https://metamask.io/download.htm)**.
- Once installed, connect Metamask to the Binance Smart Chain by following Binance's official guide at **https://academy.binance.com/en/articles/connecting-metamask-to-binance-smart-chain**.

**PolkadotJS wallet**:

- This wallet is available as an extension on both Chrome and Firefox browsers.
- Install the PolkadotJS wallet extension for **[Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd)** or **[Firefox](https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/polkadot-js-extension/)**.
- Once installed, follow the prompts to create or import your wallet.


<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [BNB staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/BNB?tokenStandard=Native) and click on the "Stake" button to stake your ETH.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbhowtostake_1.png)

### Input staking amount

To stake BNB, you will need to provide the desired amount of BNB to be staked and then click on the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbhowtostake_2.png)

### Confirm transaction

After clicking the "Stake" button, you will be redirected to your MetaMask wallet. In your MetaMask wallet, you will see a confirmation screen that shows the amount of BNB you are about to stake and the associated gas fee. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of BNB. If you are satisfied with the information, click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbhowtostake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

Congratulations! Your BNB staking process has been successfully completed. You will now earn rewards for staking your BNB.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbhowtostake_4.png)

### Add rBNB to your wallet

Navigate to the rBNB information page and click on the "+" symbol. In the Metamask interaction window, select "Add token" to successfully include rBNB in your wallet.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbhowtostake_5.png)

</Steps>

## How to Unstake

<Callout type="info">
rBNB mint and burn in StaFi Chain, if you want to unstake
</Callout>

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [BNB staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/BNB?tokenStandard=Native) and click on the "Unstake" button to stake your ETH.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbunstake_1.png)

### Input unstaking amount

To stake BNB, you will need to provide the desired amount of BNB to be staked and then click on the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbunstake_2.png)

### Confirm transaction

Provide your password and sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbunstake_3.png)

### Waiting for completed

Once unstaking is completed, the operation will be processed successfully.

![StaFi Staked BNB](/image/rbnbunstake_4.png)

</Steps>
