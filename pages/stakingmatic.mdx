## How to Stake MATIC with StaFi

### Related Wallets

- Metamask extension: https://metamask.io/download.htm
- Polkadotjs wallet: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/polkadot%7Bjs%7D-extension/mopnmbcafieddcagagdcbnhejhlodfdd(Chrome)

Please refer to this link for the guide of adding the above wallets: https://docs.stafi.io/rtoken-app/rasset/the-guide-of-metamask-and-polkadotjs-wallet

<Steps>

### Go to staking page

Go to the [MATIC staking page](https://app.stafi.io/rtoken/stake/MATIC?tokenStandard=Native) and click on the "Stake" button to stake your MATIC.

![StaFi Staked MATIC](/image/rmatichowtostake_1.webp)

### Input staking amount

To stake MATIC, you will need to provide the desired amount of MATIC to be staked and then click on the "Stake" button.

![StaFi Staked MATIC](/image/rbnbhowtostake_2.png)

### Confirm transaction

After clicking the "Stake" button, you will be redirected to your MetaMask wallet. In your MetaMask wallet, you will see a confirmation screen that shows the amount of MATIC you are about to stake and the associated gas fee. Please review the information carefully and make sure that you are staking the correct amount of MATIC. If you are satisfied with the information, click the "Confirm" button to sign the transaction.

![StaFi Staked MATIC](/image/rmatichowtostake_3.webp)

### Waiting for completed

Congratulations! Your MATIC staking process has been successfully completed. You will now earn rewards for staking your MATIC.

![StaFi Staked MATIC](/image/rmatichowtostake_4.png)

### Add rMATIC to your wallet

Navigate to the rMATIC information page and click on the "+" symbol. In the Metamask interaction window, select "Add token" to successfully include rMATIC in your wallet.

![StaFi Staked MATIC](/image/rmatichowtostake_5.png)

</Steps>
